How many videos will I get?

It's difficult to say. How long it takes to edit a video is entirely dependent on complexity, length, availability of assets, number of revisions, rendering time... You get the point. There's a lot that goes into creating custom, high-quality video content. Usually after two-weeks of working together, you'll start to find a groove with your editor and develop a sense for what's possible. We train all of our editors to communicate timeline changes early and often.

Do I really get a full-time editor?

Yes! Your editor works exclusively with you full-time at 40 hours per week. ViralCuts (through our parent company, Landed) handles all of the recruiting, training, payroll, benefits, and compliance. For a flat monthly-fee you get an amazing, full-time editor and none of the headaches.

Can my editor turn my long-form videos into shorts?

Absolutely! We typically recommend providing your editor with talking points, or better yet, timestamps, that you want converted into short-form clips.

What if it’s not a fit with my editor?

We’re confident you’ll love working with your editor. In cases where it’s not a fit, we offer a Guaranteed Replacement, which takes around 2 weeks on average. This may take longer if you’re working with someone specialized in a specific-style or format.

What if I want more than one editor?

You can hire as many editors as you need! At any time you’re able to request additional talent for your team. Each new editor is assigned with a 90-day commitment.

Do you have other roles to help our marketing team?

Yes! Our parent company, Landed, can embed practically any creative or marketing-focused role. Learn more here: talentlanded.com

Do you offer trials or make example videos?

Before placing your editor, we’ll share sample videos that they have previously made. This will help you get a feel for their quality and style. We do not however offer any trials or create example videos of your content.

Can I cancel if I don’t like it?

All of our hires begin with a 90-day commitment with Guaranteed Replacement if you’re unhappy with your editor. We’re confident you’ll love working with ViralCuts. In the event you wish to cancel, we have a 30-day cancellation policy.
